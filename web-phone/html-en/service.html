Aggregate payment

In recent years, with the rapid development of China's payment service market and the innovation and activity, relevant market players have focused on consumer demand. The product has been welcomed by consumers and effectively promoted the healthy and rapid development of non-cash payment tools in China. At the same time, merchants are faced with the need to accept consumer bank card, QR codes of different institutions, flash payment, and even more innovative payment methods.
